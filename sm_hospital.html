About Us

About S M Hospital

S M Hospital in Nayandahalli, Bengaluru provides reliable healthcare services for families in the surrounding areas. The hospital focuses on quality treatment, patient comfort, and affordable healthcare.

With experienced doctors and supportive medical staff, S M Hospital offers consultation, emergency care, and inpatient facilities. Our goal is to deliver accessible and trusted healthcare to the community.

Quality Treatment

Evidence-based medical care with focus on patient outcomes and recovery.

Affordable Care

Transparent and affordable pricing to make healthcare accessible to all.

Community Focused

Serving the Nayandahalli and Mysore Road community with dedicated care.

Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare services designed to meet all your medical needs under one roof.

General Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment for common illnesses, fever, infections, and chronic conditions.

Child Care

Dedicated paediatric care for infants and children including vaccinations and growth monitoring.

Emergency Care

Round-the-clock emergency services for accidents, sudden illness, and critical conditions.

Inpatient Treatment

Health Checkups
